Projects

Audi: An Ambient Interface with Automatic Accuracy Feedback for Crowdsourcing Platforms

Xinru Tang, Dongyang Zhao, Ying Zhang and Xianghua Ding

Current crowdsourcing platforms genrally lack accuracy feedback to users, which largely make a negative user experience. Regarding this, we designed a new interface using the background color as the feedback of accuracy. We also conducted controlled experiment to test the effect of our interface. This work was published in the exploratory paper track of ECSCW2019 (conference ranked as CCF-B).

Beyond Entertainment: A User Study among Older Adults on Short Video Platforms

Xinru Tang, Xianghua Ding et al.

Short video platforms (e.g. TikTok, KuaiShou) have been extremely popular in these years. Many research has explored topics including engaging elements of short video streaming, the demographic of video streaming users etc. In these work, researchers generally regard younger people as the targeted users of these application. However, we found a large number of older adults active on these platforms. To fill this research gap, we conduct a user study based on semi-structured interviews with 14 older users, and spotlight the older adluts' unique practices on these short video platforms. This work was submitted to the main track of CSCW2020 (conference ranked as CCF-A).
