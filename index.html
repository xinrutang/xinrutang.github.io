About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Department of Informatics, University of California, Irvine. I am advised by Dr. Anne Marie Piper as a member of Accessibility Research Collective. I was also fourtunate to work with Dr. Yixin Zou in her Human-Centered Security and Privacy group at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy (MPI-SP).

Research Interests

I'm interested in how to acknowledge and incorporate people's lived experiences into technology development, especially those coming from people as living bodies or developed within communities. My perspective has been influenced by my work with individuals or communities across disabilities, ages, languages, and cultures. I take insights across disciplines to shape my ideas.

My current focus is how to ground the design of assistive technologies into the everyday, living world of disability and push accessibility in practice.
