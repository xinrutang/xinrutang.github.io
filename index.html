About

I'll start my Ph.D. study at Department of Informatics, University of California, Irvine from 2023 Fall. I am lucky to be advised by Dr. Anne Marie Piper. I am part of Accessibility Research Collective. I am also closely working with Dr. Yixin Zou as a visiting intern on inclusive privacy at Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy (MPI-SP).

Research Interests

My research aims to create more inclusive and supportive digital experience, with a particular focus on aging and disability. I am mainly interested in:
1) how to better achieve inclusion in research and technology design, and
2) designing technology to create a more accessible world using a human-centered approach.
