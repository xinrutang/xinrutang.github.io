Project 1 - an Auto-Feedback Display to Improve Crowd Workers' User Experience

Tag: Technical HCI, User Study, Crowdsourcing

While feedback, by experts or peers, is found to have positive effects on crowdsourcing work, it is costly. We design an automatic feedback display called AuDi to try to solve this problem.

Publication: Xinru Tang, Dongyang Zhao, Ying Zhang and Xianghua Ding. AuDi: an Auto-Feedback Display for Crowdsourcing[C]//Proceedings of 17th European Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work. Project 2 - Understanding why Chinese Older Adults are Active on Short Video Platforms

Tag: Empirical Study, Qualitative Research, User Study, Interview, Tech for Older Adults

Older adults are generally regarded as resistant to new technologies. However, contrary to this general impression, recently we have noticed that a large number of older adults in China are actively contributing content on short video platforms such as DouYin and KuaiShou. Why older adults are so passionate about short videos platforms?

Project 3 - an Exploratory Analysis of Depressed Users' Posts and Interaction Pattern on Sina Weibo

Tag: Computational Social Science, Social Network Analysis, Natural Language Processing

A large number of depressed users are using social media for self-expression/self-disclosure. What can we know from their posts and behavior on social media? In this project, I employed text analysis and social network analysis techniques to explore the text posted by younger depressed users, and the interaction pattern of them on Sina Weibo (one of the most popular social media in China).

Project 4 - Exploring How Foodshow could be Integrated into Viewer's Daily Life

Tag: Empirical Study, Qualitative Research, User Study, Interview, Health Informatics

Foodshow, as a genre of user-generated video (UGV) featuring diverse activities around food, such as eating, cooking, visiting restaurants, though seemingly mundane, attracts millions of people in China. Why so many people are enjoying watching such mundane activities everyday?
